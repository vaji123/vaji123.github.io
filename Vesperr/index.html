Children's Behavioral Pattern Analysis Through Smart Toys

We are a group of undergraduate students from Sri Lanka Institute of Information Technology who specialize in Data Science stream. This is based on the final year research which we have conducted.

Introduction

Children below age five has a rapid growth in every aspect of their life. Some children in this range of age might not reach these milestones at the expected level which will lead to development disorders that affects the growth of the child. Detecting these conditions at an early age help medical professionals to treat these children efficiently. While many use different techniques for this purpose, this research focuses on using IoT based toys to analyze the motor skills of the children. Different toys were using sensors to capture movement data of arm, hand, wrist and fingers to analyze and find any atypical behavior among children.

Research Gap

There are many IoT technologies to analyze children behavioral pattern but our iot based toys are cheaper and gives reliable result. We have designed four different kinds of toys for different age group of kids by considering different types movement and activities in hand to identify behavioral pattern. This is an absolute IOT toys and we have not used any video to determine for face expression of kids.

Research Problems

We are trying to solve the problem as to how to use IoT based toys to analyze behavioral patterns of children. We collect data from different toys and collect them all into one data storage. Later analyze them using model to understand behavioral patterns. This will be helpful for experts in this field as this is not much time consuming and easier than other methods. Arm wrap and wrist bangle is easy for children to wear or play with. By using these lower and upper arm movements data will be taken for analysis.

Mainly this system will be focused on questions as follows:

  • How is the pattern of the behavior of the child?
  • What decision medical staff can make based on these patterns?
  • How to find behavioral patterns of children without affecting their natural behavior?
  • Difficulty in identifying the hand movements and rotations using economical camera
  • How to protect children’s rights for privacy ?​
  • How to get parents’ consent to participate their children in the research easily?
  • How to implement an economic solution?

Main Objective

The main objective is to identify the behavioral patterns of children. The behavioral patterns according to the data obtained from IoT toys. When a child plays with the arm wrap, wrist bangle , toy car, spoon and rattles equipped with IoT sensors and modules the data will be collected and analyzed to identify behavioral patterns.

This study uses a variety of toys to determine children's behaviors :​

Arm movements or rotations​

Finger movements and wrist movements​

Upper arm and lower arm movement​

Behavior when given a toy vehicle with wheels

Sub Objectives

Designing and developing a toy car to measure and evaluate the child’s use of the toy car and any repetitive behavior.

Designing and developing a marker and spoon to assess the child’s use of a marker for writing and a spoon for feeding. ​​

Designing and developing a bangle and arm wrap to evaluate the child’s upper and lower arm movements while playing.

Designing and developing a rattle to assess the child’s wrist movements while playing with the rattle. ​
